Nicole Walkenhorst

A creative who loves to craft a good story.

About

Nicole is a Utah based designer with over 15 years experience. She’s worked on a variety of in-house brands for non-profits ranging from university to a global church. She has a unique talent at seeing the big picture potential in a product or brand and then bringing it to life.

Each company Nicole has worked for has carried a strong mission behind life long learning, understanding, and passing along what you know to help others learn and grow. She tries to keep that same spirit for doing good in her personal life as well. She strongly believes that design and storytelling is a powerful medium to create change.

Nicole is comfortable working on a range of projects from from designing websites and apps to collaborating and creating children's magazines. Whether she’s art directing, playing with typography, or crafting prototypes, Nicole always brings a quality of work and creativity that is hard to match.

Outside of work, Nicole finds inspiration through being in the great outdoors, spending time with her husband and children, and traveling to new places. Her hunger and curiosity for life drives her to experience everything and document all that it has to offer. This thirst for life is evident in all of her work. Nicole doesn’t find a strong line between creativity, work, and play.

Skills

User Research

I have a user centered approach to design and problem solving. I start with user research to understand their needs and painpoints. Then I prototype a solution and test, iterate until it's the perfect solution for them. I think of the user in my product design and let the user data drive the design.

Product Design

I design user friendly, inclusive and accessible products. I also work directly with development teams to deliver useful design specifications: user flows, mockups, design systems, interaction guidelines, etc. I use critical thinking design skills when approaching a project that exemplify my technical skills.

Art Direction

I oversee and manage the visual aspects of projects, ensuring that the creative vision is executed successfully in both print, and digital channels. I manage projects, delegate tasks, manage budgets, and timelines. I also provide feedback and direction to the creative team to ensure that the project is completed successfully, is of the highest quality, and delivers the desired message effectively.
